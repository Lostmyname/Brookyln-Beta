    Life is an adventure. We all know that. It’s fun, surprising, occasionally scary, mostly marvellous, and intensely personal.

    Our book is a celebration of that. It’s the story of a child who has lost his or her name, and who sets off a magical journey to track down the missing letters.

    Along the way they meet lots of weird, wonderful and wise characters. And each gives the child the first letter of its name. Are you called EMILY? Okay! You’ll meet an Elephant, Mermaid, Inuit, Lion and Yeti!

    So the beauty of the book is that each child’s name creates its own special, personalised tale. Showing that we’re all different. And awesome.

    Jam-packed with beautiful illustrations and wonderful stories, the books are specially created for every child. Digital technology and traditional storytelling, perfectly in synch. Yippee!

    For Brooklyn Beta, we want to give the first 100 registered visitors their own tale – a book created for a child you think is just fabulous.
